why this page exists
OBSCURA is free, open source, and ad-free. It runs on Cloudflare and the bills get paid out of pocket. There's no paid tier, no upsell, and nothing in the app is gated behind a tip. If the project saved you a step — or you just want to keep zero-knowledge tools in the world — these are the ways to chip in.

One-off tip via card or Apple/Google Pay. Leave a note if you want — I read them.
Donations go to project upkeep: Cloudflare egress, domain renewal, the next round of security review, and the time to keep building. They are not tax-deductible — OBSCURA is not a registered nonprofit.

privacy of donations
Tips on Buy Me a Coffee are processed by Buy Me a Coffee and Stripe — they see whatever payment data your card / wallet exposes. OBSCURA does not link tips to your shares; the app has no accounts and the tip platform is on a separate domain.
